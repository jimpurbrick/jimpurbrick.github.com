Brighton Digital Festival

 Wed 04 September 2013 by Jim Purbrick

The Brighton Digital Festival starts this week and I’m very happy to be helping out with Facebook London‘s contributions: Parse By The Sea, a mobile app Hackathon featuring Parse on the 26th of September, and helping to Connect The Brighton Digital Festival by sponsoring Metranet to provide high speed internet connectivity and improved WiFi infrastructure in the Brighton Dome. If you’d like to come to the hackathon, please send an email to brightonhack@fb.com and tell us about your mobile apps. I’m looking forward to seeing lots of great hacks at Parse By The Sea and to finally get online to blog, tweet and post from dConstruct and the Brighton Miki Maker Faire this year. I hope to see you there.
